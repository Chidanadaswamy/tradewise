# 👤 Watchlist Feature - User Guide

## Getting Started with the Watchlist

The watchlist helps you **save and monitor stocks** you're interested in without buying them immediately.

---

## 📖 How to Use the Watchlist

### Step 1: Go to the Trade View
1. Click the **Trade** tab in the navigation menu
2. You'll see a list of available stocks to trade

```
📊 Trade Stocks
├─ AAPL - Apple Inc.        $195.50    +2.45%
├─ MSFT - Microsoft Corp    $420.75    -1.20%
├─ GOOGL - Google Inc.      $185.30    +0.50%
└─ ... more stocks
```

---

### Step 2: Add a Stock to Your Watchlist

**How to Add:**
1. Find a stock you want to monitor (e.g., AAPL)
2. Click the **bookmark icon** ☐ next to the stock
3. You'll see a success message: **"AAPL added to watchlist"** ✓

```
AAPL - Apple Inc.    $195.50    +2.45%    [☐ → ☑]  ← Click bookmark
```

**What happens:**
- The bookmark icon fills in ☑ (showing it's saved)
- Stock is added to your watchlist
- You can start monitoring its price

---

### Step 3: View Your Watchlist

**Method 1: Watchlist Tab**
1. Click the **Watchlist** tab in the menu
2. See all your saved stocks in one place

```
📌 My Watchlist
├─ AAPL - Apple Inc.
│  Price: $195.50
│  Daily Change: +2.45%
│  Added: Today
│
├─ MSFT - Microsoft Corp
│  Price: $420.75
│  Daily Change: -1.20%
│  Added: Yesterday
│
└─ ...more stocks
```

---

### Step 4: Monitor Live Prices

Your watchlist automatically **updates every 5 seconds** with:
- ✅ Current stock price
- ✅ Daily price change (%)
- ✅ When you added it

The prices will change in real-time as the market moves.

---

### Step 5: Remove from Watchlist

**How to Remove (2 Options):**

**Option A: From Trade View**
- Click the filled bookmark icon ☑ to unfill it ☐
- Stock is removed

**Option B: From Watchlist View**
- Click the **X button** on the stock card
- Confirm the removal
- Stock is removed

## 💡 Common Tasks

### I want to quickly buy/sell a stock from my watchlist
1. Go to **Watchlist** tab
2. Click **[Trade]** button on the stock
3. Goes to Trade view with that stock selected
4. Place your buy/sell order

### I want to add multiple stocks to watch
1. Go to **Trade** tab
2. Click bookmark on each stock one by one
3. Success messages appear for each
4. Go to **Watchlist** to see them all together

### I want to see the price trend
1. Click on any stock in watchlist
2. Goes to Trade view with that stock selected
3. See the detailed chart
4. Current and historical prices

### I want to compare multiple stocks
1. Add several stocks to watchlist
2. View Watchlist tab
3. See all prices side-by-side
4. Easy to compare

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Can I add more than 10 stocks to watchlist?
**A:** Yes! You can add any stock from the Trade view. Your watchlist can grow as large as you want.

### Q: Will my watchlist be saved if I log out?
**A:** Yes! When you log back in, all your watchlist stocks will be there.

### Q: Can I have multiple watchlists?
**A:** Currently, you have one watchlist. All saved stocks go into the same list.

### Q: What if a stock I'm watching goes down a lot?
**A:** You can still see it in your watchlist. The percentage shows you the daily change. You can remove it whenever you want.

### Q: Can I add a stock that doesn't exist?
**A:** No, you can only add stocks that are available in the app (the 10 stocks shown in Trade view).

### Q: How often does the price update?
**A:** Every 5 seconds automatically. No manual refresh needed.

### Q: Can I see the price history?
**A:** Yes! Click on a stock from your watchlist to go to Trade view and see the chart.

### Q: What does the bookmark icon mean?
**A:** 
- ☐ Empty bookmark = Not in watchlist
- ☑ Filled bookmark = In your watchlist
